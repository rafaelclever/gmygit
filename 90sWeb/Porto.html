Porto Seguro - Brazil

Porto Seguro is one of the most popular destinations in Bahia, a State of Brazil. It is a city full of history, culture and natural beauty, which also houses the famous districts of Arraial d’Ajuda, Trancoso and Caraiva. Porto Seguro’s top of mind to Brazilian people is its musicality – specifically a rhythm called Axé –, open-air concerts and destination to graduation trips.

Porto Seguro is a place that teaches us a lot about the colonization of Brazil, and it also has several beautiful beaches, indigenous reserves, parks where we can find giant Pau-Brasil trees, beach clubs and even all-inclusive resorts.

There is still much more to do in Porto Seguro! Take advantage of this same trip to spend a few days in the neighboring districts or even make a round trip to enjoy the beautiful beaches of Trancoso, the excitement and fun of Arraial d’Ajuda (make sure to check out Arraial Eco Park), or the river meeting the sea in Caraíva.
